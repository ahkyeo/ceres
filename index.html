— LOS ANGELES —

Ceres is a collective of music and zine fanatics who came out of Southern California's DIY scene. We keep our idealist spirit alive as a worker run small operation that runs affordable events in Los Angeles. It is our steadfast objective to stay inclusive, accessible, and affordable for fans and artists of all cultural, subcultural, age and socioeconomic backgrounds. Our goal is to ensure access to multi-disciplinary music, arts, and social empowerment while sustaining a diverse, non-corporate, and collaboratively run all ages music and arts scene in Los Angeles.

We’re currently busy assisting our friends at Pehrspace to re-open a physical location again as a fully operational, legal and affordable all ages venue and non-profit for the arts. Contact us anyway to see if we can help you in the mean time!
